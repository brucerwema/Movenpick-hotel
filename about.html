History of Mövenpick

Founded in Switzerland in 1948, Mövenpick began with a passion for quality, hospitality, and culinary excellence. Over the decades, the brand has grown into an internationally respected name, known for its dedication to premium ingredients and unforgettable dining experiences.

At Mövenpick Restaurant, we honor tradition while embracing modern flavors, delivering dishes crafted with care, precision, and pride. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every meal we serve, every guest we welcome, and every moment we create.

With a presence in over 40 countries and a legacy spanning more than 75 years, Mövenpick continues to set the standard for exceptional dining experiences worldwide. Our Rwanda location brings this rich heritage to the heart of Africa, combining Swiss precision with local warmth and hospitality.
